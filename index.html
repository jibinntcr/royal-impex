Our Story

Royal Impex LLC

At Royal Impex LLC, we take immense pride in our commitment to delivering exceptional products and services. With years of experience under our belt, we have developed strong relationships with reputed suppliers and manufacturers worldwide. This enables us to source and export high-quality glass products, mirrors, apples, kiwis, fresh dates, urea, and bitumen to meet the diverse needs of our clients. In addition to our exporting capabilities, we also specialize in the import of green cavendish bananas, pineapples, and coconuts. These tropical delights are carefully selected and imported from reliable growers, ensuring that we deliver fresh and delectable fruits to our customers.

Products

Explore our wide range of high-quality glass products at Royal Impex LLC. We offer an extensive selection that caters to various industries, including construction, automotive, interior design, and more.

Architectural Glass

Our architectural glass collection includes toughened glass, laminated glass, and insulated glass units (IGUs). These products are widely used in commercial and residential buildings, providing safety, energy efficiency, and aesthetic appeal.

Automotive Glass

We supply automotive glass for all types of vehicles, ranging from cars and trucks to buses and heavy machinery. Our automotive glass products meet industry standards for safety, durability, and visual clarity.

Decorative Glass

Add a touch of elegance and uniqueness to any space with our decorative glass range. From patterned glass and frosted glass to stained glass and custom designs, we offer a variety of options to suit your aesthetic preferences.

Mirrors

Enhance your interiors with our exquisite mirrors, available in various shapes, sizes, and styles. Whether you need wall mirrors, bathroom mirrors, or decorative mirrors, our collection is designed to meet your requirements.

We also provide specialized glass solutions for specific applications, such as solar glass, fire-resistant glass, bulletproof glass, and more. These products are engineered to meet the highest standards of performance, safety, and functionality.
